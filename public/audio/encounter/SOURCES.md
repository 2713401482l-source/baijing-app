# 微定自然声素材来源

本目录中的自然声均由 Wikimedia Commons 的公有领域文件转换为适合移动网页播放的 MP3。转换仅包含响度整理、频段保护和编码格式调整。

- `rain-window.mp3` — [Rain against the window.ogg](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Rain_against_the_window.ogg)，Public domain
- `rain-thunder.mp3` — [Rain and thunder.ogg](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Rain_and_thunder.ogg)，Public domain
- `campfire.mp3` — [Bones breaking wood fire ice crackling.ogg](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Bones_breaking_wood_fire_ice_crackling.ogg)，Public domain
- `pebble-waves.mp3` — [On a pebble beach.ogg](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:On_a_pebble_beach.ogg)，Public domain
- `shallow-stream.mp3` — [Shallow small river with stony riverbed.ogg](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Shallow_small_river_with_stony_riverbed.ogg)，Public domain
- `forest-ambience.mp3` — [20090610 0 ambience.ogg](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:20090610_0_ambience.ogg)，Public domain
